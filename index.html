NUMBER #2 Best Nintendo Game

-Mario Kart 8 Deluxe-

Mario Kart 8 Deluxe is a racing game in the Mario Kart series for the Nintendo Switch which was first released on April 28, 2017. The game has many powerups that allow the player, if used correctly, to come from last place, all the way to top 5. The player can race against their friends or online against random people!
